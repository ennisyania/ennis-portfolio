Name Card Design

This name card is something I designed to represent me as a person and the feeling I want to convey to people with whom I want to make connections in my career. This name card was made using Canva, but I used my own layout. I decided to keep the color mostly red, using a yellowish beige to add a pop of color where needed to emphasize certain elements.
The front of the name card features a simple layout with my logo in the middle, creating a sense of focus on it and drawing the most attention, as it is balanced right in the center. The back of the card mainly required layout planning, ensuring there were margins within sections to provide some breathing room for better readability.
This project taught me how to use color to emphasize what needs emphasis and the importance of layout to ensure readability.

City in Nature EDM

In this project, I was tasked with making an EDM for one of the Singapore Green Plan pillars. In this EDM, I utilized the design rule of emphasis to capture the viewer's attention with the bright yellow flower. This was entirely done using Illustrator, completely by me. From this project, I have learned the importance of colour schemes, as it was quite difficult for me to choose colours that blended well together and did not disrupt readability.
